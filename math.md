On a fundamental frequency when you draw it out on a closed string, you have two nodes. it is the fundamental, so it only has one antinode.
$$ L = 1/2 \lambda $$
$$ \therefore \lambda = 2L $$
Since $ v = f \lambda$, 
$$ f = \frac{v}{2 \cdot L} $$
Or,
$$ v = \frac{1}{2} \cdot {v}{L}$$
This is the fundamental frequency.

To find it with multiple bins with overlapping harmonics, the fundamental frequency is equal to the difference between bins [n] and [n-1]

For example, between the third and the fourth:
$$ f = \frac{v}{\frac{2L}{3}} $$
$$ f = \frac{v}{\frac{L}{2}} $$

Subtracting the two, you end up with:

$$ \Delta f = \frac{2V}{L} - \frac{3V}{2L} $$
$$ = \frac{4V - 3V}{2L} $$
$$ = \frac{V}{2L} $$
$$ = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{V}{L} $$
